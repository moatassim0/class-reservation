Welcome to our BMI Calculator!
The Body Mass Index Calculator is a valuable tool designed to help you assess your body composition effectively, by providing your height and weight into the calculator you can determine your index, which is a numerical value that provides insight into your overall health status based on your body size.
Why BMI though ?
It is the most accurate tool available that can categorize your body type within a certain range relative to your health.
