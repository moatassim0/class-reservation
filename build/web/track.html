Your personalized tool for monitoring and managing your daily caloric burning, with our calorie tracker you can effortlessly keep track of the calories you are burning through all the possible physical activities that are listed here using the concept of MET (Metabolic Equivalent of Task).
But First, What are MET values ?
MET values are a way to quantify the energy cost of physical activities. They represent the ratio of the rate of energy expended during an activity to the rate of energy expended at rest, as each excercise has its own estimated values, they describe the relative intensity of each activity.
